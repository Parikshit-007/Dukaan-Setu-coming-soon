Account Deletion and Data Removal

Dukaan Setu

You can request deletion of your Dukaan Setu account and associated personal data at any time. Follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Open the app and go to Profile → Account → Delete Account, or
  2. Email us at support@dukaansetu.com with the subject “Delete my account” from your registered email/phone.

What we delete

  • Account profile (name, email/phone, saved addresses)
  • Favorites, bookmarks, cart data
  • Push notification tokens linked to your user

What we may retain

  • Order records and invoices needed for accounting, tax, fraud prevention, or legal compliance
  • Server logs and aggregated analytics that do not personally identify you

Retention for the above is limited to what is required by law or legitimate business purposes. Typical retention for invoices and tax records is up to 25 days before archival or anonymization, unless a longer period is legally required in your jurisdiction.

Verification

We may ask you to verify your identity (for example, via OTP/email confirmation) to protect your account from unauthorized deletion.

Timeline

We begin processing your request immediately and complete deletion of eligible data within 25 days. Records retained for legal reasons are archived and no longer used for product features.

About Dukaan Setu

Dukaan Setu helps customers discover nearby shops, place orders, and receive updates in a fast, reliable experience. We take privacy seriously and are committed to giving you clear controls over your data, including easy account deletion.
